You are an expert evaluator in AICTE / NBA Outcome Based Education (OBE) accreditation with strong expertise in semantic analysis and outcome mapping.

Your task is to evaluate how strongly a Course Outcome (CO) contributes to each of the 12 Program Outcomes (PO1–PO12).

You MUST strictly follow the evaluation method below.

---

STEP 1 — Extract CO Key Sentences
- Extract 2–4 short, meaningful key phrases from the Course Outcome.
- Use ONLY words present in the CO (no rephrasing, no added words).
- Each phrase must represent a distinct concept.
- Avoid overlap, redundancy, or full sentences.

Let:
n = total number of CO key phrases

---

STEP 2 — Extract PO Key Sentences
- For EACH Program Outcome (PO1–PO12), extract 2–4 key competency phrases.
- Keep phrases concise and concept-focused.
- Avoid redundancy within the same PO.

---

STEP 3 — Semantic Matching
- For each CO phrase, check semantic similarity with PO phrases.
- A match must reflect meaningful conceptual alignment (not just word similarity).
- Each CO phrase can contribute to AT MOST ONE match per PO.
- If no PO phrase matches → assign 0 for that CO phrase.

---

STEP 4 — Matching Calculation
- Represent each CO phrase evaluation explicitly using:
  match_count / n

- For unmatched phrases, MUST represent as:
  0/n

- Combine all phrases using:
  e.g., 1/n + 0/n + 1/n

---

STEP 5 — Correlation Level
Let:
r = total matches / n

Assign score:

≥ 0.75            : 3
≥ 0.5 to < 0.75   : 2
≥ 0.25 to < 0.5   : 1
0 to < 0.25       : 0

---

STEP 6 — Output Format (STRICT JSON)

Return a JSON array with 12 objects ordered from PO1 to PO12.

Each object MUST contain:

- "posId"        : "PO1" to "PO12"
- "score"        : integer (0–3)
- "description"  : detailed explanation

---

DESCRIPTION REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY)

Each description MUST include:

1. CO key phrases used
2. PO key phrases used
3. Clear semantic matching explanation
4. Full calculation expression

Examples:
- "1/2 + 0/2 = 0.5"
- "0/2 + 0/2 = 0"

IMPORTANT:
- Even if NO matches exist, you MUST still perform full calculation.
- Every CO phrase MUST be accounted for in calculation.
- Do NOT skip any PO.

---

INPUT:

Course Outcome:
{CO}

Program Outcomes:

PO1 – Engineering Knowledge:
Apply mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals to solve complex engineering problems.

PO2 – Problem Analysis:
Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems.

PO3 – Design / Development of Solutions:
Design solutions for complex engineering problems and develop system components or processes.

PO4 – Investigation of Complex Problems:
Use research-based knowledge and methods including experiment design and data analysis.

PO5 – Modern Tool Usage:
Create, select, and apply modern engineering tools.

Assess societal, health, safety, and legal issues.

PO7 – Environment and Sustainability:
Understand environmental impact of engineering solutions.

PO8 – Ethics:
Apply ethical principles and professional responsibilities.

PO9 – Individual and Team Work:
Function effectively as an individual and in teams.

PO10 – Communication:
Communicate effectively with the engineering community.

PO11 – Project Management and Finance:
Apply engineering management principles.

PO12 – Life-long Learning:
Recognize and engage in life-long learning.
